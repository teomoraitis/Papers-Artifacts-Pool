Paper Summary by Theodoros Moraitis: 
**Going Green for Less Green: Optimizing the Cost of Reducing Cloud Carbon Emissions [ASPLOS'24]** (Resource management)

The Problem: Reducing the carbon footprint of batch workloads in hybrid cloud settings.

The Challenge: Carbon-aware scheduling conflicts with traditional performance and cost objectives.

High-level Solution: Carbon-aware scheduling that considers the trade-offs between carbon, performance, and cost.

Key-insights:
- Carbon-aware computing is not free.
- "Good" Trade-off points exist, and users should be able to navigate them.
